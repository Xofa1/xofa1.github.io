Cthulhu Attacks

  • 2019
  • 5
  • Unity
  • C#
  • FMOD
  • Microsoft HoloLens

Immerse yourself in a chilling augmented reality experience within Cthulhu Attacks, where your surroundings become the battleground against Lovecraftian horrors. Utilizing the Microsoft HoloLens, scan your environment to generate a game area tailored to your surroundings. As portals unleash waves of otherworldly creatures, arm yourself with mystical spells activated through hand gestures, seamlessly swapping between them with voice commands. Survive the onslaught to face the ultimate challenge: the monstrous Cthulhu. Only by vanquishing this ancient terror can you emerge victorious in this thrilling AR shooter.

Own Experience

Project Lead

As the project lead for a university project, I managed a team of five programmers and artists to develop an Unity-based augmented reality shooter using the Microsoft HoloLens. I created and assigned tasks, openly communicated progress and ideas in weekly scrum meetings, and ensured that deadlines were met.

Sound Design

I was also responsible for sound design and implementation in the game. Using the audio middleware FMOD, I integrated sounds for the player's spells and the monsters. It was important to give the floating enemies distinct sounds to make the player aware of their location, even when they were not visible in the narrow field of view of the HoloLens.
