Papers

  • Influence On Player Perception And Behavior Through Sound In Games

    September 2023, Bachelor's thesis, in German

    With ongoing research in the field of game sound, sound is evolving from the concept that sound is only responsible for supporting the visual aspect of games. This paper aims to show how integral sound design is to the development of future video games. It first establishes definitions of sound in games and then examines the effects of how sound can influence player perception and behavior. With reference to similar studies in the field, a study was developed along with an associated sample game and conducted with 26 participants. The results of the study are discussed and compared with known results of related research, and approaches for future research are presented.
